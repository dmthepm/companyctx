Seaside Dental Clinic

Seaside Dental Clinic is a dental clinic in Austin, TX. Founded 2010. We're a team of 17.

Why Austin chooses us

For more than 16 years Seaside Dental Clinic has served Austin and the surrounding TX region with dependable, locally-operated dental clinic work. Our team of 17 knows the neighborhoods, the buildings, and the seasonal rhythms that shape what our clients actually need — the kind of practical knowledge you only build by showing up in the same zip codes year after year.

Who we serve

We work with homeowners, small businesses, and repeat commercial accounts across Austin. Whether you've worked with a dental clinic before or this is your first project, we walk every new client through the process step by step: what to expect, what options exist at your price point, and what we'd recommend if the job were on our own property. No hard sell, no upsell theatre, no contracts you can't cancel.
