Cornerstone Bakery

Cornerstone Bakery is a bakery in Portland, OR. Founded 2019. We're a team of 6.

Why Portland chooses us

For more than 7 years Cornerstone Bakery has served Portland and the surrounding OR region with dependable, locally-operated bakery work. Our team of 6 knows the neighborhoods, the buildings, and the seasonal rhythms that shape what our clients actually need — the kind of practical knowledge you only build by showing up in the same zip codes year after year.

Who we serve

We work with homeowners, small businesses, and repeat commercial accounts across Portland. Whether you've worked with a bakery before or this is your first project, we walk every new client through the process step by step: what to expect, what options exist at your price point, and what we'd recommend if the job were on our own property. No hard sell, no upsell theatre, no contracts you can't cancel.

What we do

Our core offerings cover custom cakes, catering, wholesale bread, and pastry boxes. Every engagement starts with a site walkthrough and a written scope so there are no surprises on invoice day.

  • Custom cakes
  • Catering
  • Wholesale bread
  • Pastry boxes

Credentials & proof

Cornerstone Bakery is fully insured and holds every permit and certification the state of OR requires for our trade. Our team of 6 collectively brings decades of hands-on experience, and we publish before-and-after galleries, customer reviews, and project case studies so prospective clients can see the actual work — not just marketing renders.
