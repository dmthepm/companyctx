our growth over the years

Northarlington Pharmacy 01 has seen many changes since 1964. The original pharmacy was located at 100 Main St. the Owner’s son, a graduate of pharmacy school, took over leadership of the store in 1992. Guided by the traditions of service and care instilled by the founder, the Owner grew Northarlington Pharmacy 01 into what it is today. The store was expanded in the mid-1990s and forged a new path in 1999 by returning to its pharmacy roots with a prescription compounding lab. In January 2001, the store moved to a larger facility at 100 Main St, with an expanded compounding laboratory and a parking lot. It was also at this time that the store assumed its current name of Northarlington Pharmacy 01.

award winning pharmacy

In 2024, Northarlington Pharmacy 01 once again earned recognition on both a local and national level by winning the Good Neighbor Pharmacy of the Year Award. This is the second time Northarlington Pharmacy 01 has received this award, the first time back in 2016. These awards were created to recognize independent pharmacies, with over 5,000 pharmacies in contention. In 2016, we earned a Proclamation and key to the town of North Arlington to thank us for our service to the community, plus an award from the State of New Jersey for our service to the wider community. In 2017, the Owner the Owner Independent Pharmacist of the Year for the state of NJ.

3rd generation family business

Also in 2016, a third generation joined the family business, adding sports medicine, certified personal training, and precision nutrition coaching to the pharmacy’s offerings. This allowed us to continue the founder’s legacy of preventative and proactive care for our patients by adding nutrition, micronutrient testing, fitness, and natural products to the pharmacy. Through the website, we now help people from all around the United States.

Welcoming Our Newest Partner

the Owner joined Northarlington Pharmacy 01 as a pharmacy extern, became a full-time pharmacist shortly after, and has been an integral part of the business and the community ever since. Today the Owner is the Pharmacy Manager and Functional Wellness Expert — and is also a part-owner.

For over 50 years, Northarlington Pharmacy 01 and now the Owner’s Best® have carried on the traditions, service, and legacy of the Owner. We have been your friendly, family-owned and operated neighborhood pharmacy and have maintained our values and level of service as our neighborhood continues to grow.

We understand that, especially in today’s world of healthcare, trust is something that is earned. We thank you for placing your trust in us today and hope to continue to earn your trust in the future.
